Addons (and click on "Uninstall") to uninstall it. <h3>Removing</h3> To remove this addon: <ul> <li>Uninstall it</li> <li>Go in the folder <code>/extras/addons/</code> and remove the folder <code>paypal/</code>.</li> </ul> </div> </body> </html>
